Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

Westheimer Animal Clinic Bellaire ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our clients and their pets. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, mailing address, and other contact details you provide when scheduling appointments, filling out forms, or contacting us.
  • • Pet Information: Your pet's name, species, breed, age, medical history, and treatment records.
  • • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our website, including IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on our site.

How We Use Your Information

  • •To provide veterinary care and services for your pets
  • •To schedule and manage appointments
  • •To communicate with you about your pet's care, including appointment reminders and follow-ups
  • •To process payments and billing
  • •To improve our website and services
  • •To comply with legal obligations

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

  • •Veterinary specialists or emergency clinics when referrals are necessary for your pet's care
  • •Service providers who assist us in operating our website and conducting our business
  • •Legal authorities when required by law

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can set your browser to refuse cookies, though some features of our website may not function properly without them.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • •Access the personal information we hold about you
  • •Request correction of inaccurate information
  • •Request deletion of your personal information
  • •Opt out of marketing communications

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
