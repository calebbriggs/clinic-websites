Welcome to Westheimer Animal Clinic Bellaire

Our entire healthcare team is committed to providing personal attention to the unique concerns of each individual pet owner. Thank you for taking the time to read about our veterinary practice and the services we offer.

Westheimer Animal Clinic Bellaire in Bellaire, TX is a full service companion animal hospital. It is our commitment to provide quality veterinary care throughout the life of your pet. Our services and facilities are designed to assist in routine preventive care for young, healthy pets; early detection and treatment of disease as your pet ages; and complete medical and surgical care as necessary during his or her lifetime.

Full-Service Veterinary Care. Love Included.

We are a full-service companion animal hospital. Dr. Michael Huddleston and his staff are here for your family and offer all aspects of care.

Routine Vaccination Care

Keeping your pet healthy with routine vaccinations, flea and tick prevention, and heartworm prevention.

Surgery

Dr. Huddleston has over 35 years of surgical experience, from spay/neuters to advanced heart surgery with the latest equipment.

Dental Care

Professional dental care and prevention to keep your pet's teeth and gums healthy throughout their life.

Digital Imaging & Lab

Digital images, ultrasounds, MRI and CT scans with experienced staff to diagnose and recommend effective treatment.

Hospital Services

Full-service Hospital Care, Intensive Care, and Rehabilitation Centers for overnight stays and recovery.

Orthopedic Services

Arthritis management, ACL surgery, joint care, and prosthetic limb fitting to keep your pet mobile and active.

Bathing & Grooming

Professional grooming services to keep your pet looking and feeling their best.

Microchipping

Permanent identification to help reunite you with your pet if they ever get lost.

K Laser Treatments

Advanced laser therapy to reduce pain, decrease inflammation, and speed healing for your pet.

Experienced Surgical Care

Dr. Huddleston is a veterinary surgeon with over 35 years of experience. He has performed hundreds of surgeries from the most simple such as spay/neuters or cyst removal to the most advanced including heart surgery.

His meticulously clean facilities have all the latest equipment and his trained support staff are there for the safest anesthesia and operation monitoring.

Digital Imaging & Lab Services

With the latest technology and procedures we can pinpoint problems and provide medications right here in our facility.

Our staff are experienced and trained to read digital images, ultrasounds, MRI and CT scans and use their experience to diagnose and recommend effective treatment.
